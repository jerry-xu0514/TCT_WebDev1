Welcome to the Team!

Our Project is Elarm, a mobile security app.

It allows you to:

• Call 911 with one button

• Send tracking info to friends when in danger

• Activate routine check-ins to ensure safety

The Problem we are trying to solve is human trafficking.

• Each year, 840,000 children in the US go missing.

• We decided to target colleges and universities because there are many people.

• There are also other main problems that are specific to colleges and universities.
